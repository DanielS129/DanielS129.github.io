Final Project

Hello, this page contains my final project which is called the "Impossible Duck Game". In this game you play as a
a small white duck trying to get through the obstacles in its way. What you need to do is press the space key to enable
your duck to jump. The game is considered impossible as its very dificult to get the duck through the obstacles as you need
time your jumps very accurately.
